Our Premium Car Care Services

At Autohunk, we offer a wide range of professional auto detailing and car wash services to meet your vehicle’s needs. From restoring your car’s interior to protecting its exterior, we guarantee top-notch results every time.

Full Auto Detailing

A complete cleaning and restoration of your vehicle inside and out. Our detailing experts make sure every inch of your car looks and feels brand new.
  • Exterior hand wash and waxing.
  • Interior vacuuming and deep cleaning.
  • Tire and rim care.

Exterior Wash & Wax

Keep your car sparkling with our thorough exterior wash and protective wax application.
  • Hand washing and drying.
  • Bug and tar removal.
  • Long-lasting wax for a glossy finish.

Interior Detailing

Revitalize your car’s interior with our deep cleaning services tailored for all surfaces.
  • Carpet shampooing and deodorizing.
  • Leather cleaning and conditioning.
  • Dashboard and console polish.

Ceramic Coating

Protect your car’s paint with our high-performance ceramic coating for a lasting shine and durability.
  • Hydrophobic and anti-scratch layer.
  • UV protection.
  • Enhances paint gloss and depth.

Engine Cleaning

A spotless engine bay for better performance and a cleaner appearance.
  • Safe degreasing techniques.
  • Dirt and grime removal.
  • Protection of sensitive engine components.
